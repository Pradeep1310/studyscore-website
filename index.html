Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About StudyScore

What is StudyScore?

StudyScore is India's first independent, student-powered rating system for study abroad agents and overseas education consultants. Think of it as CIBIL for education agents — a transparent, data-driven quality score based on real student experiences. We are not affiliated with any education agency.

Is my survey response anonymous?

Yes, 100% anonymous. We do not collect your name, email, or any identifying information. No study abroad agent will ever know who submitted a specific response. Your privacy is absolute.

Who can fill the StudyScore survey?

Any student who has used a study abroad agent or overseas education consultant to apply for a student visa to any country — Canada, UK, Australia, USA, Ireland, Germany, New Zealand, or any other destination.

How is StudyScore different from Google Reviews?

Google Reviews can be manipulated — agents often ask staff and friends to post fake positive reviews. StudyScore collects structured, anonymous feedback on specific parameters like fee transparency, communication quality, and post-visa support. The data is aggregated across hundreds of responses to create a reliable quality score that cannot be gamed by agents.

Is StudyScore affiliated with any education agency?

No. StudyScore is completely independent. We are not affiliated with, sponsored by, or funded by any education agent, university, or recruitment company. Our only stakeholder is the student and their family.

How will study abroad agents be rated?

Each education agent receives a StudyScore from 0 to 100 based on verified student feedback across five parameters: fee transparency (20%), communication quality (15%), visa support quality (25%), post-visa support (10%), and overall student satisfaction (30%). Higher scores indicate better student experiences.
